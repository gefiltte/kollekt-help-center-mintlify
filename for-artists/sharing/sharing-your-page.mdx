# Sharing Your Kollekt Page

Built-in sharing tools on every screen of your Kollekt page. Share links to your Home page, Direct Line, or Chat — each link lands the visitor directly on the section you choose. Share via Instagram Stories, WhatsApp, Messages, or any app on your phone.

## Share Icon Location

A **share icon** (curved arrow) appears in the top-left area of every main screen: Home, Direct Line, and Chat. Tapping it opens the Share Sheet. The URL that gets copied changes depending on which screen you're on, so the visitor lands exactly where you want them.

## Share Sheet

Tapping the share icon on any screen opens the **Share Sheet** — a bottom modal with three actions.

![Share Sheet with preview card](screenshots/sharing-preview-sheet.png)

**What you'll see:** A dimmed background with a bottom sheet overlay. At the top: "Preview" label. Below: a branded **Kollekt Card** showing the artist's photo on a black-and-white geometric background with the "KOLLEKT K" logo. Two primary action buttons: **Share story** (Instagram icon) and **Copy link** (link icon). Below those: "or" divider text and an **Other options** button (share arrow icon).

### Copy Link

Tapping **Copy link** copies the URL for the current screen to your clipboard. The URL changes depending on which screen you opened the Share Sheet from:

- **Home** → copies your artist page link (e.g., `https://kollekt.io/klara`)
- **Direct Line** → copies the Direct Line link (e.g., `https://kollekt.io/klara/chat/direct-line`)
- **Chat** → copies the Chat link (e.g., `https://kollekt.io/klara/chat`)

This means visitors land exactly on the section you want them to see.

### Other Options (Native Share Sheet)

Tapping **Other options** opens the device's native share sheet (iOS or Android). From here you can share the link directly via WhatsApp, Messages, Mail, AirDrop, Instagram, or any other app installed on your phone.

## Sharing to Instagram Stories

The Share Sheet includes a dedicated **Share story** button for posting your Kollekt Card directly to Instagram Stories. Here's the full flow:

### Step 1: Copy the Link First

Before tapping Share story, tap **Copy link** first. The link needs to be on your clipboard because Instagram doesn't always carry it over automatically.

### Step 2: Tap Share Story

Tap the **Share story** button (Instagram icon). Instagram opens with the Kollekt Card already placed as a story sticker — the branded image with your photo and the KOLLEKT logo appears on the story canvas.

![Kollekt Card in Instagram Stories](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-sticker.png)

**What you'll see:** Instagram Stories editor. Top-left: "< Kollekt" back link and X close button. Top-right: text (Aa), sticker, effects, music, and more icons. Center: the Kollekt Card with the artist photo and "KOLLEKT K" branding on the geometric background. Bottom: "Add a caption..." text field. Footer: "Your story" and "Close Friends" share targets with a blue arrow send button.

### Step 3: Add the Link Sticker

Tap the **sticker icon** in the Instagram toolbar and select the **Link** sticker. Paste the Kollekt URL from your clipboard into the URL field.

![Adding link in Instagram](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-add-kollekt-link.png)

**What you'll see:** Instagram "Add link" screen. Top bar: "Cancel" (left), "Add link" title (center), "Done" (right). URL field contains `https://kollekt.io/klara/chat/direct-line`. Helper text: "People who view your story can tap the sticker to visit this link." Below: "+ Customise sticker text" option. The keyboard is open for editing.

> **Tip:** On some devices the link may disappear from your clipboard when switching to Instagram. If this happens, go back to Kollekt, copy the link again, and paste it into the Link sticker.

### Step 4: Post the Story

Once the link sticker is added, it appears on top of the Kollekt Card. Post the story as usual.

![Final story with link sticker](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-kollekt-link-added.png)

**What you'll see:** Instagram Stories editor with the Kollekt Card displayed. A white **link sticker** reading "🔗 KOLLEKT.IO" is placed over the artist photo. Top-right: text, sticker, effects, music, and more icons. Bottom: "Add a caption..." text field. Footer: "Your story" and "Close Friends" share targets with a blue arrow send button.

## Quick Reference

| What you want to share | Where to tap the share icon | Where the visitor lands |
|---|---|---|
| Your artist profile | Home screen | Artist Home page |
| Your Direct Line | Direct Line screen | Direct Line feed |
| Your Chat | Chat screen | Community Chat |

## Known Limitations

- The link must be copied manually before sharing to Instagram Stories — it is not automatically embedded in the story.
- On some devices, the clipboard content may be cleared when switching from Kollekt to Instagram, requiring the user to go back and re-copy.
- The Kollekt Card image (used for Instagram Stories) is generated automatically and cannot be customized by the artist.
- The native share sheet (Other options) appearance varies by operating system and installed apps.
