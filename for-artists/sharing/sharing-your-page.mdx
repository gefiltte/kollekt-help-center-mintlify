Your Kollekt link is only useful if fans know where to find it. This is the list of places that actually move the needle, in rough order of leverage.

## What to know first

- **The in-app Share button is in the top-left of every main screen.** Home, Direct Line, Chat. The URL it generates changes based on which screen you're on, so the visitor lands exactly where you want them.
- **Three URL types** depending on where you tap Share:
  - Home → your artist page (`https://kollekt.io/yourname`)
  - Direct Line → lands the visitor in your Direct Line
  - Chat → lands the visitor in your Chat
- **Keep Kollekt as your primary link.** Your Kollekt Home already shows your social links, merch, tickets, and music — so it works as a single link-hub. The advantage is that every fan who taps it lands in your space and becomes reachable. If you can only use one link, use Kollekt.

## The in-app Share button

Tap the **share icon** (curved arrow) in the top-left of Home, Direct Line, or Chat to open the Share Sheet.

![Artist profile page with share icon](screenshots/sharing-artistpage-screen.png)

The Share Sheet has three actions:

![Share Sheet with preview card](screenshots/sharing-preview-sheet.png)

- **Copy link** — copies the URL for the current screen to your clipboard. Paste anywhere.
- **Share story** — posts your branded Kollekt Card directly to Instagram Stories.
- **Other options** — opens your device's native share sheet (WhatsApp, Messages, Mail, AirDrop, any other app).

## Instagram bio

The highest-leverage single placement. Your bio is seen by every fan who lands on your profile.

1. Open Instagram, go to your profile, tap **Edit profile**.
2. Find the **Links** section and tap **Add link**.
3. Paste `https://app.kollekt.io/yourname` as the URL.
4. Set the label to `Kollekt` or `@yourname on Kollekt`. Keep it short.
5. If you have multiple links, drag Kollekt to the top.
6. Tap **Done**.

**Tips:**
- Use your handle or the word "Kollekt" as the label, not "Click here." It reads as a destination, not a CTA.
- Pair the bio text with what you're currently promoting. "New song in the Direct Line — tap below" converts better than a generic "Join my Kollekt."
- A pinned post can explain what the bio link actually is, since the label itself can't.

## Instagram Stories

30-second mechanic. Your Kollekt Card gets posted as the Story background, and the Link sticker carries the actual URL.

### 1. Copy the link first

Before tapping **Share story**, tap **Copy link**. The URL needs to be on your clipboard because Instagram doesn't always carry it over automatically.

### 2. Tap Share story

Instagram opens with the Kollekt Card already placed as your Story background.

![Kollekt Card in Instagram Stories](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-sticker.png)

### 3. Add the Link sticker

Tap the **sticker icon** in the Instagram toolbar, select the **Link** sticker, and paste the Kollekt URL from your clipboard.

![Adding link in Instagram](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-add-kollekt-link.png)

<Tip>
On some iOS devices the link disappears from your clipboard when switching to Instagram. If that happens, go back to Kollekt, copy again, and paste.
</Tip>

### 4. Post the story

![Final story with link sticker](screenshots/sharing-external-ig-story-kollekt-link-added.png)

## TikTok

- **Bio link.** TikTok allows one link in your bio once you have 1,000+ followers. Use the same rule as Instagram: put Kollekt there, label it clearly.
- **Pinned video.** Pin a short video that tells fans what they'll get if they tap through ("Get all my stuff in one place — link in bio").
- **Captions.** You can't put clickable links in TikTok captions, but you can write `app.kollekt.io/yourname` so fans can type it manually when a post goes viral.

## YouTube

- **Pinned comment** under every video that includes your Kollekt link. Pinned comments stay at the top.
- **Description.** Put Kollekt as the first link in every video description.
- **Channel About page.** Add Kollekt under Links.
- **End card.** Use YouTube's end-card feature to link out to your Kollekt page in the last 20 seconds of any video.

## Live shows

- **QR code on stage.** Kollekt generates a QR for your page — put it on the video screens between songs, on the stage backdrop, or on the merch table.
- **Stage banter.** "Go to Kollekt dot io slash my name — I post everything there first." Repeating it once per show is enough.
- **Setlist insert or flyer.** Print a small card with the QR and a line ("Get my updates before anyone else.").
- **Merch table.** Every purchase is a chance to point fans to Kollekt. A small sign next to the card reader works.

## Anywhere else

- **Email signature.** Every email you send is a share opportunity. "Kollekt: app.kollekt.io/yourname" under your name.
- **Newsletter.** If you already run a newsletter, put Kollekt at the top of every issue.
- **Spotify release description.** When you drop a track, put Kollekt in the pre-save/release description.
- **Press kit.** Every EPK should include your Kollekt URL so journalists and bookers can see the community.

## Signs it's working

Check your Kollekt stats regularly. A spike in new members after a Story tells you the IG link worked. A steady stream from Instagram is the bio link. A post-show bump is live-show sharing working. Each surface has a signature pattern — once you see it, you know what to double down on.
