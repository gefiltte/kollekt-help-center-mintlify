This is the place where you can find everything about using Kollekt as an artist.

Kollekt is where music artists reach their fans directly. One page at `kollekt.io/yourname`, one link anyone can join from, no algorithm between you and your fans.

If it's your first time, start with [Claim your page](/for-artists/start-here/claim-your-page) — it walks you through verifying it's you and taking ownership of your pre-built Kollekt space.

If you can't find an answer here, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) or DM [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) on Instagram.
