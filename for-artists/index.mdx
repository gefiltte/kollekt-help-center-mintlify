## New here? Claim your page.

Almost every artist on Kollekt already has a page waiting — pre-populated with your name, cover photo, and social links. Start at [app.kollekt.io/verify-artist](https://app.kollekt.io/verify-artist). Verification is a one-word Instagram DM to [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) — full instructions walk you through it from there.

[See the full walk-through →](/for-artists/start-here/claim-your-page)
