# Attach photos, videos, and audio

<Warning>
**This article is a stub.** Full walk-through coming soon.
</Warning>

Direct Line messages can include photos, videos, and audio — alone or with a text caption. Every message still sends one push notification.

## Attachment options

Tap the **+** button in the compose bar to reveal attachment options.

![Attachment options expanded](screenshots/directline-attachment-options.png)

**What you'll see:** Three circular icons with labels: **Camera** (take a photo), **Media** (pick from library), **Audio** (pick an audio file).

## What you can attach

- **Photos** — one or many. Multiple photos show as a grid in the feed.
- **Videos** — appears with a play button overlay. Can include a text caption.
- **Voice notes** — via the **microphone** icon in the compose bar.
- **Audio files** — via the **Audio** option in the **+** menu.

## Multiple photos

Messages can include multiple photos. Thumbnails appear as a horizontal row in the compose area with a count badge.

![Composing with multiple photos](screenshots/directline-composer-multi-photo.png)

**What you'll see:** A row of **photo thumbnails** with a **badge reading "5"** indicating five photos attached.
