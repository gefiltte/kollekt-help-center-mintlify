Direct Line is a one-way broadcast feed. Every message sends a push notification to every fan in your community. You can share text, photos, videos, and voice messages. Messages can be marked subscriber-only.

## Your Direct Line feed

Direct Line is accessed via the second tab in the bottom navigation bar (paper plane icon). The feed displays messages in reverse-chronological order — newest at the bottom.

### Empty state

![Empty Direct Line view](screenshots/directline-empty.png)

**What you'll see:** Top-left shows your community name ("Klara"). Below: the artist avatar with "Direct Line" label, and three action icons to the right — share, send (paper plane), and chat bubble. The message input ("Send a message...") sits at the bottom with a **+** button, **diamond icon**, **microphone icon**, and **send arrow**.

### Feed with messages

![Single text message in feed](screenshots/directline-text-message.png)

**What you'll see:** A message from "Klara" with avatar, purple name, timestamp, and a **···** menu. Heart icon on the right side.

## Compose a text message

Tap the message input field to open the keyboard. Type your message and tap the **send arrow** to post it.

![Composing a text message](screenshots/directline-composer-text.png)

## Attach media

For attaching photos, videos, and audio to your message, see [Attach photos, videos, and audio](/for-artists/direct-line/attach-media).

![Attachment options expanded](screenshots/directline-attachment-options.png)

## Record a voice message

Tap the **microphone icon** in the compose bar to start recording. A recording interface appears over the feed.

![Voice message recording in progress](screenshots/directline-voice-message-recording.png)

**What you'll see:** A **recording timer** reading "0:02" with a large **red microphone button** (pulsing) below it.

### Review and send

After stopping the recording, a review interface appears with playback controls.

![Voice recording review](screenshots/directline-voice-recording-overview.png)

**What you'll see:** A **play button** (triangle) on the left, an **audio waveform** in the center, and a **duration** on the right. Below: a **red trash icon** (delete) and a **green checkmark** (confirm and send).

## Subscriber-only content

To lock a post so only subscribers can see it, see [Post subscriber-only content](/for-artists/direct-line/post-subscriber-only-content).

![Subscriber-only mode activated](screenshots/directline-subscriber-only-toggle.png)

## Delete a message

Tap the **···** (three-dot menu) on any message to reveal the **Delete Message** option.

![Delete message option](screenshots/directline-delete-message.png)

## Share your Direct Line

Tap the **share icon** at the top of the Direct Line screen to open the sharing panel. See [Share your Kollekt link](/for-artists/sharing/sharing-your-page).

![Sharing panel](screenshots/directline-sharing.png)

## Push notifications

Every Direct Line message sends an iOS push notification to all community members (or subscribers only, if subscriber-only mode was used).

![Push notification banner](screenshots/directline-push-notification.png)

## Known limitations

- Whether fans can react to Direct Line messages is not shown from the artist perspective.
- The exact behavior of the quote block below video messages is not fully demonstrated.
- Whether subscriber-only messages show a visual lock to non-subscribers is not shown from the artist side.
