Direct Line is your one-way broadcast to every fan who's joined your space. Every message lands as a push notification — no algorithm, no feed, no guessing who saw it. Compose like you would a voice note or a caption, not a tweet thread. The feed is reverse-chronological — newest messages are at the bottom.

Media and subscriber-only posts have their own articles. For attachments, see [Attach photos, videos, and audio](/for-artists/direct-line/attach-media). To lock a post to subscribers, see [Post subscriber-only content](/for-artists/direct-line/post-subscriber-only-content).

## Send a text message

Open Direct Line from the bottom nav — the paper-plane tab.

![Empty Direct Line view](screenshots/directline-empty.png)

1. Open Direct Line from the bottom nav.
2. Tap the message input at the bottom.
3. Type your message.
4. Tap the send arrow.

Your message appears in the feed with your avatar, your artist name, a timestamp, and a three-dot menu for editing.

## Record a voice message

Tap the microphone icon in the compose bar to start recording.

![Voice message recording in progress](screenshots/directline-voice-message-recording.png)

Stop recording when you're done. A review screen lets you play it back before sending.

![Voice recording review](screenshots/directline-voice-recording-overview.png)

Tap the green checkmark to send, or the red trash icon to discard and try again.

## Delete a message

Tap the three-dot menu on any message you've sent and choose **Delete Message**.

![Delete message option](screenshots/directline-delete-message.png)

## The push notification

Every Direct Line post triggers a push notification to every member (or to subscribers only, if you used subscriber-only mode).

![Push notification banner](screenshots/directline-push-notification.png)

## Signs it's working

- Your message shows in the Direct Line feed right after you hit send.
- Fans with the app installed see a push notification within a few seconds.
- The message count on your Stats screen goes up by one. See [See your stats and revenue](/for-artists/subscriptions/see-your-stats-and-revenue).
