Link groups are the sections on your Artist page that hold one or more links. They sit below your social icons and let you spotlight what's current — tour dates, a new release, merch, whatever matters this month.

## What to know first

- **A link group has a title and one or more links.** Each link has a title, a URL, and an optional image thumbnail.
- **Edit from the admin menu.** Three-dot menu on your Artist page, then **Edit Artist Page**, then **+ New link group** at the bottom.
- **Keep it tight.** 2 to 4 groups is the sweet spot. More than that and the page starts to feel like a list.

## Create a link group

1. Open your Artist page and tap the three-dot menu.
2. Tap **Edit Artist Page**.
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **+ New link group**.
4. Give the group a title fans recognize — **Merch**, **New music**, **Tour**, **Socials**.
5. Add one or more links. Each link gets a title, a URL, and an optional image.
6. Tap **Save** in the top right.

## Add, reorder, or remove links

Inside an existing group, you can:

- Add another link.
- Reorder links by dragging.
- Remove a single link.
- Reorder whole groups on the page.
- Delete a group entirely.

## Tips

- **Use images when you can.** Thumbnail links convert better than plain text rows.
- **Name groups by category, not by vibe.** "New music" beats "What I'm into" — fans scan, they don't read.
- **Refresh quarterly.** Old tour dates and last year's single make the page feel stale.

## Signs it's working

Your groups show on your public page at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`, each link opens the right destination, and the order matches what's most important right now.
