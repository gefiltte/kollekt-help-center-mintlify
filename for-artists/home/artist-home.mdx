Your Artist page is the main screen of the artist experience in Kollekt. It's what fans see when they visit, and it's where you jump off to Direct Line, Chat, and your admin tools. The page is public — the URL is `app.kollekt.io/yourname` and anyone with the link can land on it.

## The first view

Your personal account is separate from your Artist page. When you open the app, you land on your account home — your personal identity at the top, your artist page as a card below. Tap the card to open your Artist page.

![First view after opening the app](screenshots/home-first-view.png)

## The public Artist page

This is what fans see — cover photo, artist name, social icons, link groups, and the bottom nav bar.

![Artist Page as it appears](screenshots/home-artist-page-first-impression.png)

From here you can:

- Tap the **share icon** (top left) to open the share sheet. See [Share your Kollekt link](/for-artists/sharing/sharing-your-page).
- Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right) to open the admin menu.
- Use the bottom nav to move between Home, Direct Line, Chat, and your profile.

## The admin menu

The three-dot menu on the top right of your Artist page opens controls that fans don't see. Tap it to open the admin menu — it shows your Members and Subscribers counts at the top, then four options:

![Admin menu open](screenshots/home-admin-menu.png)

- **Edit Artist Page** — change your cover photo, name, socials, and link groups. See [Edit your Artist page](/for-artists/home/editing-the-artist-page).
- **Stats** — earnings, members, subscribers, messages. See [See your stats and revenue](/for-artists/subscriptions/see-your-stats-and-revenue).
- **Payments** — subscription price and revenue splits. See [Add team members and split revenue](/for-artists/admin/team-members-and-revenue-splits).
- **Roles** — add admins and moderators. See [Manage admins and roles](/for-artists/admin/manage-admins-and-roles).

## Signs it's working

Your Artist page loads cleanly at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`, the three-dot menu opens your admin tools, and the bottom nav takes you to Direct Line and Chat without a detour.
