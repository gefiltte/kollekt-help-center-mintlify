Your Artist page comes pre-populated — you're customizing, not building from scratch. This is where you swap in a cover photo you like, confirm your name, line up your socials, and add the links that matter right now.

## What to know first

- **Open the editor from the admin menu.** On your Artist page, tap the three-dot menu (top right) and choose **Edit Artist Page**.
- **Nothing is live until you save.** Edits stay local until you tap **Save** in the top right.
- **Link groups are covered separately.** For adding and organizing link groups, see [Add and organize link groups](/for-artists/home/add-and-organize-link-groups).

## The edit screen

![Edit page overview](screenshots/home-edit-overview.png)

From this screen you can change the cover photo, edit the artist name, add social icons, and add link groups. Tap **Save** when you're done.

## Change your cover photo

Tap the pencil icon on the cover photo to open the photo picker.

![Photo picker menu](screenshots/home-edit-photo-picker.png)

Pick a photo from your library, take a new one, or choose a file. The new cover shows immediately in the editor — it goes live once you save.

## Edit your artist name

Tap the pencil icon next to your artist name. The field becomes editable and your keyboard opens. Type the name you want fans to see and tap out of the field.

## Add your socials

Tap the **+** circle next to your existing social icons to open the platform picker.

![Social platform picker](screenshots/home-edit-social-picker.png)

Kollekt supports Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, Twitch, Discord, and SoundCloud. Tap a platform to add it, then paste the full profile URL. If the format is wrong, the field turns red and tells you what's off.

## Save your changes

Tap **Save** in the top right. A green **Profile updated** toast confirms the change is live.

![Save success toast](screenshots/home-edit-save-success.png)

## Signs it's working

- Your cover photo and name show on the public page at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`.
- Your social icons each open the right profile when tapped.
- The **Profile updated** toast appeared after your last save.
