Your user profile is your personal account inside Kollekt — avatar, username, bio, email, birthday, phone, country. It's your own identity inside the app, separate from your public Artist page at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`, and it's what appears when you post in Chat as yourself rather than as the artist. Payouts live here too — the **Set up payouts** section is at the bottom of the edit form. See [Connect your payout account](/for-artists/subscriptions/connect-your-payout-account). To delete your account, see [Delete or pause your account](/for-artists/user-profile/delete-or-pause-your-account).

## Profile overview

Open the profile screen from the bottom nav — the profile icon on the far right.

![Profile overview with default avatar](screenshots/userprofile-overview-default.png)

Your avatar, username, bio, and a card preview of your Artist page stack here. Tap **Edit** (the gear icon) to open the profile editor.

## Sidebar menu

Tap the hamburger icon in the top-right to open the sidebar — account switcher, artist search, help, and log out.

![Sidebar menu open](screenshots/userprofile-sidebar-menu.png)

## Edit your profile

Tap **Edit** on the profile overview to open the form.

![Edit profile form](screenshots/userprofile-edit-default.png)

The form splits into private and public fields:

- **Private.** Email (read-only), Birthday, Phone Number, Country.
- **Public.** Username, Bio.

Tap the pencil icon on the avatar to change your photo. Tap **Birthday** for a scroll-wheel date picker. Tap **Username** or **Bio** to type directly into those fields.

## Save changes

Tap **Save** at the bottom of the form. A green **Profile updated** toast confirms.

![Save success toast](screenshots/userprofile-save-success.png)

## Signs it's working

Your avatar and username show correctly on the profile overview, your bio appears in your Chat identity when you post as yourself, and the **Profile updated** toast appeared after your last save.
