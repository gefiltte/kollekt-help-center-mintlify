# Connect your payout account

<Note>
**Your earnings accrue from the first subscription, whether or not your payout account is connected.** Connect your bank and identity details whenever you're ready to withdraw — no rush at signup.
</Note>

Payouts on Kollekt are powered by Stripe. Connecting takes a few minutes and requires your bank details, a bit of ID, and your tax info.

## Where to start

Open **Edit your user profile** (tap the profile icon in the bottom-right of the nav bar, then the gear + **Edit** button). Scroll to the bottom of the edit form. You'll see a **Set up payouts** section with a cream-colored **Set up** button.

![Edit profile showing Set up payouts](screenshots/userprofile-edit-bottom.png)

Tap **Set up** to open the full-screen payout form.

## Bank details and identity

![Payout form — bank details and identity](screenshots/userprofile-payouts-1.png)

**What you'll see:** Three sections.

- **Bank details** — Account Holder Name, Bank Country, Account Number / IBAN.
- **Identity** — Legal First Name, Legal Last Name, Date Of Birth.
- **Address** (starts here, continues on next section) — Street Address.

All fields are required. Red helper text ("This field is required") appears under any blank field.

## Address and tax info

![Payout form — address and tax info](screenshots/userprofile-payouts-2.png)

**What you'll see:**

- **Address** — Street Address, City, Postal Code, Country.
- **Tax information** — Tax Identification Number (TIN), TIN Country, VAT ID (optional).

At the bottom: a full-width **Save** button.

## What happens after

Once saved, your payout account is connected. Earnings held before connection are released to your account according to Stripe's normal payout schedule.
