Payouts on Kollekt run through Stripe. Connecting takes a few minutes and needs your bank details, a bit of ID, and your tax info. You can do this any time — there's no rush at signup.

## What to know first

- **Your earnings accrue from the first subscription.** Connecting is only needed before you want to withdraw.
- **You'll need three things.** Bank details, legal ID, tax info.
- **Only the page owner sets this up.** Team members on a revenue split have their own payout connection under their own profile.
- **The schedule is Stripe's.** Once connected, earnings pay out on Stripe's normal cadence for your country.

## Open the payout form

1. Tap your profile icon (bottom-right of the nav bar).
2. Tap the gear icon with **Edit** to open your user profile editor.
3. Scroll to the bottom. You'll see a **Set up payouts** section with a cream-colored **Set up** button.
4. Tap **Set up**.

![Edit profile showing Set up payouts](/for-artists/user-profile/screenshots/userprofile-edit-bottom.png)

## Fill in bank details and identity

![Payout form — bank details and identity](/for-artists/user-profile/screenshots/userprofile-payouts-1.png)

Three sections open up:

- **Bank details.** Account Holder Name, Bank Country, Account Number or IBAN.
- **Identity.** Legal First Name, Legal Last Name, Date of Birth.
- **Address.** Starts here with Street Address and continues in the next section.

All fields are required. A red helper message appears under any blank field when you try to save.

## Fill in address and tax info

![Payout form — address and tax info](/for-artists/user-profile/screenshots/userprofile-payouts-2.png)

- **Address.** Street Address, City, Postal Code, Country.
- **Tax information.** Tax Identification Number (TIN), TIN Country, VAT ID (optional).

Tap **Save** at the bottom of the form.

## What happens after

Your payout account is connected. Any earnings held before you connected are released on Stripe's normal payout schedule — the exact timing depends on your country.

## Signs it's working

The payout form submits without errors, the **Set up payouts** section on your profile no longer shows the cream-colored button, and your first Stripe payout arrives within the normal schedule for your country.
