Stats is the one place to answer "how's my space actually doing?" — earnings, members, subscribers, Direct Line and Chat activity, and full transaction history, all filterable by date range. Numbers only, no fluff.

## The Stats overview

Open Stats from the admin menu: three-dot menu on your Artist page, then **Stats**.

![Stats overview screen](/for-artists/admin/screenshots/admin-stats-overview.png)

The stats card covers:

- **Earnings** — total revenue for the selected range.
- **Members** — current count, plus **New** and **Unfollowed** for the range.
- **Subscribers** — current count, plus **New** and **Cancelled** for the range.
- **Direct Line Messages** — count for the range.
- **Chat Messages** — count for the range.
- **Transactions** — individual payment records for the range.

## Pick a date range

Everything on Stats is filtered by date range. The pill near the top controls what the whole screen shows. Tap the date range pill to open the calendar picker.

![Calendar date picker](/for-artists/admin/screenshots/admin-stats-date-picker.png)

Select a start and end date, then tap the blue checkmark. The Stats screen updates immediately to reflect the new range.

## What to check and when

- **Weekly.** Glance at new members and new subscribers. Are you growing?
- **After a release or big post.** Change the date range to the post day and the two days after. Direct Line Messages up, members up, subscribers up — you just learned what moves the needle.
- **Monthly.** Earnings total and transaction count. Matches your own expectations? If not, something's off and worth flagging.

## Signs it's working

You can answer "how many fans joined this week?" and "what did I earn this month?" in under thirty seconds, and the transaction list matches what you'd expect based on your subscriber count.
