The Stats screen shows your earnings, community growth, messaging activity, and transaction history for any date range. Access it from the admin menu on your Artist page (⋮ → Stats).

## Stats overview

![Stats overview screen](/for-artists/admin/screenshots/admin-stats-overview.png)

**What you'll see:** Top bar: back arrow (left), "Stats" title (center), share/export icon (right). Below: "Filter by date" label with a date range pill. The stats card shows:

- **Earnings** — total revenue for the selected period.
- **Members** — current count, plus **New** and **Unfollowed** for the period.
- **Subscribers** — current count, plus **New** and **Cancelled** for the period.
- **Direct Line Messages** — count for the period.
- **Chat Messages** — count for the period.
- **Transactions** — individual payment records for the period.

## Pick a date range

Tap the date range pill to open a calendar picker.

![Calendar date picker](/for-artists/admin/screenshots/admin-stats-date-picker.png)

**What you'll see:** A full month grid (MON–SUN) with selectable start and end dates. **Reset** button (left) and a **blue checkmark** confirm button (right). Tapping the date pill again shows the exact start and end dates.

## Known limitations

- The share/export icon in the Stats top bar is visible but its behavior is not documented.
- Transaction records appear when payments exist but the exact row format is not captured.
