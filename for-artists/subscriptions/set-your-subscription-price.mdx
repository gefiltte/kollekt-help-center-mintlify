# Set your subscription price

<Warning>
**This article is a stub.** Full walk-through coming soon.
</Warning>

You can set or change what fans pay per month to subscribe to your community. Price changes apply to new subscribers; existing subscribers keep the price they signed up at.

## How to change it

1. Open the admin menu from your Artist page (⋮ in the top right).
2. Tap **Payments**.
3. Set your subscription price.
4. Save.

## Pricing tips

- **Start low.** €3–€5 gets early subscribers who want to support you. You can raise later for new signups.
- **Don't benchmark on Patreon.** Kollekt is simpler for the fan — the subscribe button is right there in the app.
- **Change is fine.** Raising the price doesn't affect existing subscribers, so you can experiment.
