Your subscription price is the minimum monthly amount a fan pays to subscribe. The default is €4.99, and fans can choose to pay more if they want. You can raise or lower the minimum at any time.

## What to know first

- **The price is a floor, not a fixed tier.** Fans see your minimum when they subscribe and can pay anything above it. Everyone gets the same perks regardless of amount.
- **€4.99 is the lowest you can go.** It's also the platform default.
- **Price changes apply to new subscribers only.** Existing subscribers keep the amount they signed up at.
- **Only the page owner can change the price.** Admins and moderators see a locked view on the Payments screen.

## Change your minimum price

1. Open your Artist page.
2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right) to open the admin menu.
3. Tap **Payments**.
4. Update the subscription price field.
5. Tap **Save**.

## Pricing notes

- **Start at the default.** €4.99 gets early subscribers who want to support you without overthinking it. Fans who want to give more will.
- **Raise later if it feels right.** Because existing subscribers keep their original amount, you can experiment safely.
- **The in-app subscription flow is one tap.** A lower floor matches that and helps early subscribers say yes.

## Signs it's working

Your Payments screen shows the updated minimum, new subscriptions come in at or above that amount, and your Stats show the subscriber count growing week over week.
