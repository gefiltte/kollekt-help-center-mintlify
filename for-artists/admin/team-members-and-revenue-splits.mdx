The Payments screen is where you split revenue across a team. Manager, label, a featured collaborator — you assign a percentage and they get paid that share of every sale.

Open Payments from the admin menu: three-dot menu on your Artist page, then **Payments**.

## If you're not the owner

Only the page owner sees the editable view. Admins and moderators who open Payments see a locked card with a warning — payments are managed by the page owner.

![Payments locked for non-owner](screenshots/admin-payments-locked-non-owner.png)

## The owner view

You'll see two sections:

- **Earnings.** Revenue from all sales, split into **Pending** (queued for payout) and **Paid** (already transferred).
- **Default split.** How revenue divides across the team. Tap the info icon on either section for the in-app tooltip.

## Add someone to the split

The total can't exceed 100% — the remaining percentage stays with the owner.

1. Tap **Add user to split**.
2. Search for the team member by name or username.
3. Enter their percentage.
4. Tap the checkmark to confirm.

![Entering split percentage](screenshots/admin-payments-add-user-percent.png)

The row shows **Shared [percentage]%** at the bottom. Tap **Save** to lock it in.

![Split added confirmation](screenshots/admin-payments-split-added.png)

A green **Split added** banner confirms.

## Remove someone from the split

Swipe left on the team member's row or tap the delete icon. **Remove** appears. The percentage returns to 100% for the remaining owner (or redistributes automatically if there are other splits).

## Signs it's working

Your team member appears in the Default split list with the right percentage, the totals sum to 100%, and your next payout on Stripe shows the split distribution.
