The Payments screen manages how revenue is split between team members. Only the **page owner** can access and edit payment splits. Access it from your Artist page: ⋮ → **Payments**.

## Non-owner view

If you're not the page owner, you'll see a locked screen with a warning message.

![Payments locked for non-owner](screenshots/admin-payments-locked-non-owner.png)

**What you'll see:** A card with a yellow **warning triangle** and the text **"Payments are managed by the page owner."**

## Earnings and default split (owner view)

The owner sees two sections: **Earnings** (revenue from all sales, split into pending and paid) and **Default split** (how revenue divides across the team).

![Earnings info tooltip](screenshots/admin-payments-earnings-info.png)

**Earnings tooltip:** "Revenue from all sales. Pending is queued for payout. Paid has been transferred."

![Default split info tooltip](screenshots/admin-payments-default-split-info.png)

**Default split tooltip:** "How revenue is split across the team. Applies to subscriptions and all sales unless a product has its own split set below. Total can't exceed 100%."

## Add someone to the split

Tap **Add user to split** to add a team member and set their percentage.

![Entering split percentage](screenshots/admin-payments-add-user-percent.png)

**What you'll see:** The user row shows a text field for the percentage, a **checkmark** to confirm, and an **×** to cancel. Below: **Shared [percentage]%** summary and a **Save** button.

![Split editing with Remove option](screenshots/admin-payments-split-editing.png)

![Split added confirmation](screenshots/admin-payments-split-added.png)

**What you'll see:** A green **Split added** banner with "Added [username] as payment recipient."

## Remove someone from the split

Swipe left on a team member's row (or tap the delete icon) to reveal **Remove**.

![Split removed confirmation](screenshots/admin-payments-split-removed.png)

**What you'll see:** A green **Split removed** banner. The percentage returns to 100% for the remaining owner.

## Known limitations

- Only the page owner sees Payments — admins and moderators see the locked message.
- Per-product splits are referenced in the tooltip but not demonstrated here.
