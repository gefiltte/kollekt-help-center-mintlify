The Roles screen is where you bring people onto your team with the right level of access. Three tiers — Owner, Admins, and Moderators — each with a different scope of what they can see and do.

## What to know first

- **Open Roles from the admin menu.** Three-dot menu on your Artist page, then **Roles**.
- **The Owner is fixed.** One per space, and you can't reassign it from this screen.
- **Admins get admin panel access.** Stats and Roles, but not Payments.
- **Moderators get Chat tools.** Timeout, promote, remove members. See [Moderate your chat](/for-artists/chat/moderate-your-chat).
- **Payments is owner-only.** Revenue splits and subscription price stay with the page owner.

## The Roles screen

![Roles screen with all tiers](screenshots/admin-roles-empty-moderators.png)

Three sections stack vertically:

- **Owner.** A single row with avatar, name, and email.
- **Admins.** Current admins with a delete icon per row, plus an **Add admin** button.
- **Moderators.** Current moderators (empty to start), plus an **Add moderator** button.

## Add a moderator

1. Tap **Add moderator**.
2. Type a name or username in the search field.
3. Tap a result to add them.

![Searching for a moderator](screenshots/admin-roles-add-moderator-search.png)

A green **Moderator added** banner confirms.

![Moderator added confirmation](screenshots/admin-roles-moderator-added.png)

## Remove a moderator

Swipe left on the moderator's row, or tap the delete icon. Confirm **Remove**. A green banner confirms the removal.

## Who can do what

- **Admins** — admin panel access (Stats, Roles). No Payments, no revenue split edits.
- **Moderators** — Chat moderation: timeout, promote, remove members.
- **Owner** — everything, including Payments (revenue splits and subscription price).

## Signs it's working

Your admins can open Stats and Roles without seeing a locked screen, your moderators can timeout and promote in Chat, and Payments remains accessible only to you as the owner.
