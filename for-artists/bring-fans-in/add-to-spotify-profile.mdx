Spotify for Artists lets you add external links directly to your public artist profile. This is one of the highest-leverage placements for your Kollekt link: every fan who lands on your Spotify page sees it before they hear a note.

## What to know first

- **You need access to Spotify for Artists** (`artists.spotify.com`). If you haven't claimed your Spotify artist profile yet, do that first. It's free and takes a few days to approve.
- **Spotify allows up to 5 external links** in your profile. Kollekt fits well as a permanent slot. Rotate in tour dates, merch, and releases around it as needed.
- **The link is visible on mobile and desktop.** Mobile is where most fans discover new artists, which is also where this link will get the most taps.

## The steps

1. **Go to [artists.spotify.com](https://artists.spotify.com)** and sign in.
2. **Open your artist profile**, then the **Profile** tab.
3. **Find the External Links section** (sometimes labeled "Artist Picks" or under "Profile customization" — Spotify occasionally moves it).
4. **Add a new link:**
   - Label: `Kollekt` or `@yourname on Kollekt`
   - URL: `https://app.kollekt.io/yourname`
5. **Save and publish.** The change goes live on your public profile within a few minutes.

## Signs it's working

- Your link appears as a button on your public Spotify artist profile, visible to every listener
- New members arriving via Spotify show up in your Kollekt stats over the next days and weeks

## Tips

- **Use your handle or the word "Kollekt" as the label** rather than "Click here to subscribe." It reads as an identity marker, not a CTA, which converts better.
- **If you only have room for one link**, choose Kollekt over Linktree or a merch store. Kollekt captures every fan into your space. The others lose them after the click.
