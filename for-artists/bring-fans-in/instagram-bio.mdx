Your Instagram bio is seen by every fan who lands on your profile. Putting your Kollekt link there is the single highest-leverage placement for it. Takes about a minute.

## What to know first

- **Instagram allows up to 5 bio links.** If you only use one, make it Kollekt. If you use several, keep Kollekt at the top.
- **Kollekt over Linktree.** A Linktree is a middle page that adds a click between your fan and the thing they actually want. Your Kollekt page does the same job (social links, merch, tickets, music) AND captures every fan into your space along the way. If you can only keep one, keep Kollekt.
- **The link and the bio text work together.** A good bio tells the fan what they'll get if they tap through ("Get my updates. Subscribe. Hang in the chat." etc.).

## The steps

1. Open the **Instagram app** and go to your profile.
2. Tap **Edit profile**.
3. Find the **Links** section and tap **Add link**.
4. Paste `https://app.kollekt.io/yourname` as the URL.
5. Set the label to **`Kollekt`** or `@yourname on Kollekt`. Keep it short.
6. If you already have links listed, drag Kollekt to the top.
7. Tap **Done** to save.

That's the mechanic. The whole thing is reversible — you can update, reorder, or remove links any time.

## Signs it's working

Check your Kollekt stats over the next few days. You'll see new members arriving from Instagram. The bio link typically drives the steadiest stream of new members because it's seen every time someone lands on your profile.

## Tips

- **Use your handle or "Kollekt" as the label**, not "Click here." It reads as a destination, not a call to action, which converts better.
- **Match the bio text to the moment.** If you're about to drop a new track, something like "New song in the Direct Line — tap below" works better than a generic "Join my Kollekt." The more specific the promise, the higher the tap-through.
- **Pin a post that points to Kollekt.** The bio link is static. A pinned post can say "new members get X in their first week" and explains what the bio link actually is.
