Your Instagram bio is seen by every fan who lands on your profile. Putting your Kollekt link there is the single highest-leverage placement for it. Takes about a minute.

## What to know first

- **Instagram allows up to 5 bio links.** If you only use one, make it Kollekt. If you use several, keep Kollekt at the top.
- **The bio and the link work together.** A good bio tells the fan what they'll get if they tap through ("Get my updates. Subscribe. Hang in the chat." etc.). The more specific, the higher the tap-through.
- **The Kollekt URL format** is `https://app.kollekt.io/yourname`. Use lowercase and no spaces.

## The steps

1. Open the **Instagram app** and go to your profile.
2. Tap **Edit profile**.
3. Find the **Links** section and tap **Add link**.
4. Paste `https://app.kollekt.io/yourname` as the URL.
5. Set the label to **`Kollekt`** or `@yourname on Kollekt`. Keep it short.
6. If you already have links listed, drag Kollekt to the top.
7. Tap **Done** to save.

## Signs it's working

Check your Kollekt stats over the next few days. You'll see new members arriving from Instagram. The bio link typically drives the steadiest stream of new members because it's seen every time someone lands on your profile — no timing required.

## Tips

- **Use your handle or "Kollekt" as the label**, not "Click here." It reads as a destination, not a call to action, which converts better.
- **Match your bio text to what you're currently doing.** A generic "Music + more" converts worse than "New track in the Direct Line — tap below." The more specific the promise, the more people tap.
- **Pin a post that explains what the bio link is.** The label itself is short — a pinned post can do the talking. See [Announce Kollekt to your fans](/for-artists/bring-fans-in/announce-to-your-fans) for what to actually put in the pinned post.
