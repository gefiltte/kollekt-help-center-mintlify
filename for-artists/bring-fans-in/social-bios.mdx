Your social bios are seen by every fan who lands on your profile. Putting Kollekt there is the permanent, always-on version of sharing your link. Do all of them in one sitting — block a few minutes, open your main platforms, update the bios in one pass. You'll forget otherwise. Use the same label everywhere (`Kollekt` or `@yourname on Kollekt`) — same text builds recognition, fans see it repeatedly, and it starts to mean something. The URL is `kollekt.io/yourname`, lowercase, no spaces.

## Where to put it

Add your Kollekt link anywhere fans can tap a link on your profile:

- **Instagram** — add Kollekt to your bio links. Pair with a pinned post that explains what fans will find there.
- **TikTok** — add Kollekt as your bio link, or put `kollekt.io/yourname` in the bio text and say the URL out loud in your videos.
- **Threads** — same link rules as Instagram.
- **YouTube** — three placements worth using: the channel About page, a pinned comment under every video, and the first line of every video description.
- **X / Twitter** — one clickable URL in the bio. Add a pinned tweet that explains what Kollekt is.
- **Facebook (artist page)** — set Kollekt as your primary Website link.
- **Twitch** — if you stream, add Kollekt to your Panels or profile links so viewers can land there during the stream.

## Bio text that works

Pair the link with context. Most platforms let you add a line of bio text around the link, and a short, specific line converts much better than a generic one. The line around the link matters more than the link itself. A few framings that convert better than a generic "music + more":

- **"Where I post the stuff I don't put anywhere else."**
- **"New music here first. Every time."**
- **"For the fans who actually listen."**
- **"Voice notes, unreleased things, the real stuff."**
- **"More me, less feed."**

Swap your bio text to match what's happening that week. A bio that matches the moment converts better than a static one.

## Tips

- **Re-check the bios every quarter.** Platforms move things and change link allowances. A short audit every few months keeps everything current.
- **If you only have time for one today, do the platform you post on most.** Start where your audience already is.
