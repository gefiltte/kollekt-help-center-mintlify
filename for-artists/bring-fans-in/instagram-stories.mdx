Your Instagram Story is the fastest way to put your Kollekt link in front of your followers. Kollekt drops a branded Story card into the Instagram composer for you — you add the link sticker and post. Takes about 30 seconds.

## The steps

### 1. Copy the link first

In the Kollekt app, open the Share Sheet on your Home screen and tap **Copy link**. The URL needs to be on your clipboard because Instagram doesn't always carry it over automatically.

### 2. Tap Share story

From the same Share Sheet, tap **Share story**. Instagram opens with your Kollekt Card already placed as the Story background.

![Kollekt Card loaded in Instagram Stories](screenshots/ig-story-card-loaded.png)

### 3. Add the Link sticker

Tap the **sticker icon** in the Instagram toolbar, select the **Link** sticker, and paste the Kollekt URL from your clipboard.

![Adding the Instagram link sticker](screenshots/ig-story-add-link.png)

<Tip>
On some devices the link disappears from your clipboard when switching to Instagram. If that happens, go back to Kollekt, tap **Copy link** again, and paste.
</Tip>

### 4. Post the Story

Drag the link sticker where it fits best on the card, then tap **Your Story** to post.

![Instagram Story with Kollekt link sticker in place](screenshots/ig-story-link-added.png)

## What to say on the Story

Add a line of text on top of the card so fans know why to tap. Specific beats generic — "tap through" is weaker than naming what they'll get. A few that work:

- **"New track in the Direct Line → tap to hear it first."**
- **"Voice note about [thing] on my Kollekt."**
- **"Everything I post before anywhere else → tap."**
- **"If you want the good stuff first, my space is here."**
- **"Behind-the-scenes from today → on Kollekt."**

The first time you post a Story about Kollekt, give fans a bit more context — what it is and why you're inviting them. See [Announce Kollekt to your fans](/for-artists/bring-fans-in/announce-to-your-fans) for sample launch captions.

## Tips

- **Post Stories when you have a reason.** "Hey, I'm on Kollekt now" is fine once. After that, post when you have something specific — a new Direct Line message, a subscriber-only post, a Chat moment.
- **Post when your followers are most active.** The link lives for 24 hours — drop it when the most people will see it.
- **Want a different look?** Swap in your own image before posting. The link sticker works with any Story background.
