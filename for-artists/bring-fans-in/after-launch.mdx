The announcement is 24–48 hours. The next few months are where the space stays alive or goes quiet — and the difference is almost always whether you keep posting.

## The rhythm

There's no formula, but a pattern most artists settle into:

- **Post to Direct Line a couple of times a week.** A photo and a sentence counts. A voice note counts. "Should I do this or that?" counts. Low friction beats polish.
- **When you post to Direct Line, Story about it the same day.** "New voice note on my Kollekt" with a link sticker. This is how new members keep arriving week after week.
- **Drop into Chat when you feel like it.** Heart a few messages, reply to one or two. Ten minutes is enough.
- **Don't copy-paste your Instagram content.** Fans who joined Kollekt joined for something they don't get on Instagram — voice notes, unfinished things, studio moments, the stuff you don't post anywhere else.

The cadence matters more than the volume. Fans start to expect posts and check for them.

## Loop back from your other platforms

Most new members will keep arriving from Instagram and TikTok. Every Kollekt post is a reason to Story about it. The specific reference is the hook — "Just dropped a voice note about [thing] on my Kollekt" converts better than a generic "I'm on Kollekt!"

Add one line to your next TikTok or YouTube caption too: "more of this on my Kollekt." That's enough.

## If fans aren't joining yet

A quiet first week is normal. Instagram Stories only reach a slice of your followers, and most fans need to see the announcement more than once before they tap through. Before you conclude it's not working, check:

- **Is the link in your bio live, and does it land on your Kollekt page?** Open it from an incognito browser.
- **Does your Home look ready?** Cover photo, artist name, at least one Direct Line post. If fans tapped through to an empty page, they left.
- **Did you mention what fans actually get?** "Voice notes and the stuff I don't post anywhere else" is more tappable than "join my Kollekt."
- **Did you share on more than one surface?** One Story rarely does it. Feed post, close-fan DMs, next TikTok.

If any of these are off, fix them before you re-announce.

## When to re-announce

Wait about two weeks, and re-announce when you have something specific to point at — a new track, a voice note you just dropped, a show coming up. Not a re-pitch of Kollekt itself. The stronger the specific, the higher the conversion.

## If it's still slow after a couple of weeks

- **DM 10 close fans individually.** Not a mass DM — one at a time. This is the highest-converting move you can make.
- **Audit every bio.** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X — Kollekt link should be in all of them. See [Add Kollekt to your social bios](/for-artists/bring-fans-in/social-bios).
- **Email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io).** We can look at your stats with you and suggest the next move.
