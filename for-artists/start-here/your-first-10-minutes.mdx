Once you've [claimed your page](/for-artists/start-here/claim-your-page), do these three things the first time you log in. No payout setup required. Your earnings accrue whether your bank account is connected or not. You can connect it whenever you're ready to withdraw.

## The three moves

1. **Check your cover photo and artist name.** Both come pre-populated. Update them if anything's out of date or wrong. See [Edit your Artist page](/for-artists/home/editing-the-artist-page).
2. **Send one Direct Line message to yourself.** Post a photo and a sentence to get a feel for how a broadcast reaches fans. See [Send a Direct Line message](/for-artists/direct-line/sending-messages).
3. **Copy your Kollekt link and put it in your Instagram bio.** This is the single highest-leverage share move. See [Add Kollekt to your social bios](/for-artists/bring-fans-in/social-bios).

## Signs it's working

- Your page looks like yours at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`
- You have at least one test Direct Line message posted
- Your Kollekt link is live in your Instagram bio

## What you can do later

Once the basics above are in, here's what to configure next when you're ready:

- [Add and organize link groups](/for-artists/home/add-and-organize-link-groups) — add external links (tour dates, releases, merch) beyond the social icons
- [How subscriptions and earnings work](/for-artists/subscriptions/how-subscriptions-and-earnings-work) — plan what to post subscriber-only, understand the revenue split
- [Connect your payout account](/for-artists/subscriptions/connect-your-payout-account) — needed before Stripe can release your earnings
- [Add team members and split revenue](/for-artists/admin/team-members-and-revenue-splits) — bring in your manager, label, or video team
