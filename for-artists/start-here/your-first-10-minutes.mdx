The first time you log in, do these three things. No payout setup required. Your earnings accrue whether your bank account is connected or not. You can connect it whenever you're ready to withdraw.

## What to know first

- **Your page already exists.** It comes pre-populated with your cover photo, social links, and basic artist info. You're customizing, not creating from scratch.
- **Nothing goes out until you post.** Members can join your page, but they won't see anything in their feed until you send a Direct Line message.
- **Skip the long-tail setup for now.** Link groups, subscriptions, payouts, team members can all wait. Get to "live" first.

## The three moves

1. **Check your cover photo and artist name.** Both come pre-populated. Update them if anything's out of date or wrong. See [Edit your Artist page](/for-artists/home/editing-the-artist-page).
2. **Send one Direct Line message to yourself.** Post a photo and a sentence to get a feel for how a broadcast reaches fans. See [Send a Direct Line message](/for-artists/direct-line/sending-messages).
3. **Copy your Kollekt link and put it in your Instagram bio.** This is the single highest-leverage share move. See [Add Kollekt to your social bios](/for-artists/bring-fans-in/social-bios).

## Signs it's working

- Your page looks like yours at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`
- You have at least one test Direct Line message posted
- Your Kollekt link is live in your Instagram bio
