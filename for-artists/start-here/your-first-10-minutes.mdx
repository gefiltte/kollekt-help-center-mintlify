A tight sequence to follow the first time you log in. No payout setup required — your earnings accrue regardless; you can connect your bank whenever you're ready to withdraw.

## The five moves

1. **Add a cover photo and your artist name.** See [Edit your Artist page](/for-artists/home/editing-the-artist-page).
2. **Add two link groups.** Merch, music, tour — whatever's current. See [Add and organize link groups](/for-artists/home/add-and-organize-link-groups).
3. **Send one Direct Line message to yourself.** Get a feel for how it reaches fans. See [Send a Direct Line message](/for-artists/direct-line/sending-messages).
4. **Copy your Kollekt link.** Put it in your Instagram bio. See [Share your Kollekt link](/for-artists/sharing/sharing-your-page).
5. **Tell your closest fans.** One Instagram Story, one group chat message, one email.

That's it. Your page is live.

## What you can do later

- Set a subscription price. See [Set your subscription price](/for-artists/subscriptions/set-your-subscription-price).
- Connect your payout account. See [Connect your payout account](/for-artists/subscriptions/connect-your-payout-account).
- Add team members. See [Add team members and split revenue](/for-artists/admin/team-members-and-revenue-splits).
