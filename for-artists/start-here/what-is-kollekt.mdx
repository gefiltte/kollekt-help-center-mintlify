Kollekt is an app for music artists and the fans who follow them. One page per artist, one link anyone can join from, and a direct way to reach every fan who's in, whether they found you on Spotify, Instagram, TikTok, a live show, or anywhere else.

This page is the orientation. If you're already set up and want to get going, head to [Your first hour](/for-artists/start-here/your-first-10-minutes).

## How it works

Every Kollekt page has three main parts:

- **Home** — your public page at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`. Cover photo, bio, social links, custom link sections. It replaces a Linktree.
- **Direct Line** — a one-way broadcast from you to every fan who's joined. Every message sends a push notification. Photos, videos, voice notes, text.
- **Chat** — a community conversation between your fans. They can talk to each other. You can post too.

A fourth part, **Drops**, appears when Kollekt activates it for a specific experience (an album rollout, a tour diary, a documentary series). Drops isn't self-serve yet. It's enabled per artist.

## Members and subscribers

Anyone can join your Kollekt space for free. Joining makes someone a **member**. Members get push notifications, read your Direct Line, and can talk in Chat.

**Subscribers** are members who pay a monthly amount to support you. Subscribers stay in your space either way, even if they cancel. The subscription isn't access to Kollekt. It's extras on top:

- Subscriber-only posts in Direct Line that regular members can't see
- A subscriber-only Chat room
- The ability to send images and videos in Chat
- A subscriber badge next to their name

Subscribing starts at €4.99 a month. From there, it's pay-what-you-want — every fan can choose to pay more if they want to support you further. Everyone gets the same perks regardless of the amount.

## How Kollekt is different

A few things worth knowing upfront:

- **No algorithm.** Your fans see what you post, every time. There's no feed to game.
- **No explore page.** Kollekt never shows your fans other artists. They joined for you.
- **No cross-promotion.** We don't advertise other artists to your members.
- **Revenue split.** You keep 80% of subscription revenue after VAT and Stripe fees. Kollekt takes 20%.
- **Kollekt doesn't touch streaming, touring, or merch.** We only earn from what happens on the platform.
