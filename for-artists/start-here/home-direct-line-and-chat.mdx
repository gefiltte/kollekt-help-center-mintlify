Your Kollekt space has three parts, each doing a different job. A quick map so you know where to post, talk, or launch something — and where to go for the deeper how-to on each.

## Home

Your public page at `app.kollekt.io/yourname`. It's the first thing fans see and the only part accessible without joining. Cover photo, social icons, and custom link groups for anything else — tour, merch, music, a release. See [Edit your Artist page](/for-artists/home/editing-the-artist-page) to make it yours.

![Artist Home page on Kollekt](screenshots/surface-home.png)

## Direct Line

A one-way broadcast from you to every member. Every post sends a push notification — no algorithm, no in-between. Use it like dropping a message to a close group of friends: voice notes, photos, quick text, whatever you've got. See [Send a Direct Line message](/for-artists/direct-line/sending-messages) to post your first.

![Direct Line feed with posts from the artist](screenshots/surface-direct-line.png)

## Chat

A two-way conversation between your fans. Members post, reply, react. You can drop in, but the point is fans talking to each other — it's where loyalty compounds. See [Run your community Chat](/for-artists/chat/community-chat) to set the tone.

![Chat with active community conversation](screenshots/surface-chat.png)
