Your Kollekt page already exists. To take ownership, verify it's you through a quick Instagram DM from your official artist account. Takes a few minutes.

Want to preview your page before you claim it? Go to [app.kollekt.io/verify-artist](https://app.kollekt.io/verify-artist) and search your artist name — you'll land on the pre-built Home page with an **Unclaimed** label. Otherwise, skip straight to step 1 below.

## The steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="DM @kollekt.io on Instagram">
    From your **official artist account**, open [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) and send exactly one word: **"Verify"**. No extra punctuation, no additional message. Kollekt confirms the DM came from the matching Instagram profile and replies with the next steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    After verification, a login link arrives in your email. Tap it — you'll be prompted to download the Kollekt app if you don't have it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to the app">
    Once the app opens, you're signed in automatically as the page owner. The **Unclaimed** label is gone, an admin menu appears in the bottom-right of Home, and Direct Line and Chat are editable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If verification doesn't come through

A few things to check:

- **Use your official Instagram account.** If it still doesn't go through, contact us.
- **Send exactly the word "Verify"** — no extra punctuation, no additional message.
- **Don't have access to your artist Instagram?** Email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) and we'll help you figure out a path.

If verification is still stuck, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) with your artist name and Instagram handle.
