Every Kollekt page starts already built. Your artist name, cover photo, and social links are pre-populated before you arrive. To take ownership and start using Direct Line, Chat, and subscriptions, you need to verify it's you. Verification happens through a one-word Instagram DM.

## What to know first

- **Your page is visible before you claim it.** It shows up at `app.kollekt.io/yourname` as soon as it's created. Fans can view it, but nothing new gets posted until you're in.
- **Verification happens via Instagram DM.** You send the word "Verify" to [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) on Instagram from your official artist account. We match it to your Kollekt artist identity and let you in.
- **You need access to your verified Instagram.** If you don't have the login to your main artist Instagram, verification can't go through. Someone on the team with access needs to do this step.

## The steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your page">
    Search your artist name on the Kollekt onboarding page, or tap **Find yours** inside the Kollekt app. Select your profile from the results. You'll land on your pre-built Home page with an **Unclaimed** label under your name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Slide to Join, then Claim this page">
    At the bottom of your Home, slide the **Slide to Join** bar to the right. A modal appears: *"This page hasn't been claimed yet."* Choose **Claim this page**. The other option, **Join as a member**, is for fans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify via Instagram">
    The verification screen shows the word **"Verify"** — tap it to copy to your clipboard. Open [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) on Instagram from your **official artist account**, paste, and send. Kollekt confirms the DM came from the matching Instagram profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and tap the magic link">
    Back in the Kollekt flow, enter your email address. A magic link arrives in your inbox within seconds. Tap it, and the Kollekt app opens with you logged in as the page owner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signs it's working

- The **Unclaimed** label disappears from your page
- An admin menu (three-dot icon) appears in the top-right of your Home screen
- Direct Line and Chat are now editable as the artist, not read-only

## If verification doesn't come through

A few things to check:

- **You DM'd from your official Instagram account.** Verification won't match if the DM came from a personal or fan account.
- **Your Instagram account name lines up with your Kollekt artist name.** Small variations can trip the match.
- **You sent exactly the word "Verify".** No extra punctuation, no additional message.

If you've checked all three and verification is still stuck, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) with your artist name and Instagram handle. Real humans respond during weekdays.
