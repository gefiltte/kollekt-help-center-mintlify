Every Kollekt page starts already built. Your artist name, cover photo, and social links are pre-populated before you arrive. To take ownership and start using Direct Line, Chat, and subscriptions, you need to verify it's you. Verification happens through Instagram, using your official artist account.

## What to know first

- **Your page is visible before you claim it.** It shows up at `app.kollekt.io/yourname` as soon as it's created. You can view it, but no one else can use it before you claim it.
- **Verification happens via Instagram DM.** You send "Verify" to [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) on Instagram from your official artist account. We match it to your Kollekt artist identity and let you in.
- **You need access to your verified Instagram.** If you don't have the login to your main artist Instagram, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) and we'll help you figure out a path.

## The steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your page">
    Go to [app.kollekt.io/verify-artist](https://app.kollekt.io/verify-artist) and search your artist name. Select your profile from the results. You'll land on your pre-built Home page with an **Unclaimed** label under your name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Slide to Join, then Claim this page">
    At the bottom of your Home, slide the **Slide to Join** bar to the right. A modal appears: *"This page hasn't been claimed yet."* Choose **Claim this page**. The other option, **Join as a member**, is for fans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify via Instagram">
    Open [@kollekt.io](https://instagram.com/kollekt.io) on Instagram from your **official artist account**, send us **"Verify"**, and follow the next steps. Kollekt confirms the DM came from the matching Instagram profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox and sign in">
    After verification, a login link arrives in your inbox. Tap it — you'll be prompted to download the Kollekt app if you don't have it, then the app signs you in automatically as the page owner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signs it's working

- The **Unclaimed** label disappears from your page
- An admin menu appears in the bottom-right of your Home screen
- Direct Line and Chat are now editable as the artist, not read-only

## If verification doesn't come through

A few things to check:

- **Try your official Instagram account.** If it still doesn't go through, contact us.
- **Send exactly the word "Verify".** No extra punctuation, no additional message.

If verification is still stuck, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io) with your artist name and Instagram handle.
