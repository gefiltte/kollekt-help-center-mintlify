Moderation in Chat happens one member at a time. Tap a fan's name or avatar to open their profile card, then pick the action from there. Two actions live under **Manage**: **Timeout** mutes them for a set duration, **Promote** makes them a moderator.

## Open a member's profile card

Tap the name or avatar next to any message in Chat. The card shows the fan's avatar, display name, role badge, and when they were last seen.

![Member profile card](screenshots/chat-moderation-member.png)

## Timeout a member

Tap **Timeout** under **Manage**. Four durations appear.

![Timeout duration options](screenshots/chat-moderation-timeout-options.png)

Pick one:

- **1 hour** — cooling-off for a single heated moment.
- **1 day** — stronger signal, still short.
- **7 days** — for repeat issues.
- **Permanently** — the last-resort ban.

While a timeout is active, the member's card shows **Muted** with the time remaining. Tap **Remove timeout** (the button turns red) to end it early.

## Promote a moderator

Moderators can moderate too — promoting someone extends the tools to them, useful for active rooms where you can't be online all day. Tap **Promote** to give someone moderator privileges. The card updates with a gold shield badge and the role changes to **Moderator**.

![Moderator state](screenshots/chat-moderation-moderator.png)

To revoke, open the same card and tap **Remove moderator status** at the bottom.

## Signs it's working

- Your timeouts take effect immediately — the muted member can't post until the duration ends.
- Moderators you've promoted are handling minor issues without needing you.
- The Subscribers Chat room and Main Chat both stay on-topic and friendly.
