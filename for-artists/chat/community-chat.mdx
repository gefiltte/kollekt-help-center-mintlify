Chat is the live group room inside your Kollekt space. Fans talk to each other and to you. You can post as your personal account or as your artist identity — two distinct voices in the same room.

## What to know first

- **Two rooms per space.** **Main Chat** is open to every member. **Subscribers Chat** is only for paying subscribers.
- **You have two identities.** Personal account (your own avatar) or artist identity (your artist page). You pick per message.
- **Fans can react, reply, and thread.** Chat is theirs as much as yours.
- **For moderation actions, see [Moderate your chat](/for-artists/chat/moderate-your-chat).**

## Switch between rooms

The current room name sits at the top of the screen with a dropdown arrow. Tap it to switch between Main Chat and Subscribers Chat.

![Room dropdown showing both rooms](screenshots/chat-room-dropdown.png)

## Send as personal or as artist

Tap the **+** button next to the message input. The identity pill at the bottom shows who you're currently sending as — your personal account by default.

![Identity switcher showing personal account](screenshots/chat-identity-switcher.png)

Tap the refresh icon on the pill to switch. When you're sending as your artist, the input field turns purple and reads "Sending as [Artist Name]" so you don't forget mid-thread.

![Message sent as artist](screenshots/chat-sending-as-artist.png)

## React, reply, delete

- **React.** Tap the heart icon on any message.
- **Reply.** Tap **Reply** on a message to start a thread. The reply appears below the original with a "Reply to:" label and a preview.

![Thread with replies](screenshots/chat-reply-thread.png)

- **Delete your own message.** Tap the three-dot menu on a message you sent and choose **Delete Message**.

![Delete message option](screenshots/chat-delete-message.png)

## Signs it's working

- Fans are posting in Main Chat without you starting every thread.
- The Subscribers Chat room has activity from your paying members.
- Your identity pill reads the one you want before you send — personal for casual hangs, artist when you're speaking officially.
