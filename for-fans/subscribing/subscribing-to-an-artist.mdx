# Subscribing to an Artist

Subscribing is optional. Most of the artist's Kollekt space is free — joining the community, reading the Direct Line, chatting with other fans. Subscribing unlocks extras from artists who have turned it on.

<Info>
**This article is a stub.** The end-to-end subscribe flow is being documented from video. If you're stuck, email [support@kollekt.io](mailto:support@kollekt.io).
</Info>

## What subscribing unlocks

- **Subscriber-only Direct Line posts** — content the artist has locked for subscribers.
- **Subscriber-only chat rooms** — a separate conversation for the fans who stepped up.
- **Send images and videos in chat** — where everyone else is text-only.
- **Highlighted name and subscriber badge** — visible marker in chat and profiles.

Not every artist has subscriptions turned on. If you don't see a subscribe option, the artist hasn't enabled it yet.

## How to subscribe

1. On a locked Direct Line post or subscriber-only chat room, tap the prompt to unlock.
2. Review the price and what's included.
3. Confirm payment.

You'll get access immediately.

## Managing your subscription

Open your profile (the person icon in the side menu). Your active subscriptions show up there with options to manage or cancel.
